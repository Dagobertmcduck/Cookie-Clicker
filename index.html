About Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker is one of the best online games. It is a game where you have to click on a cookie. Ultimately, you have enough cookies to buy on an autoclicker. This autoclicker will add 1 free cookie every second. The goal is to get as many cookies as possible. To speed up the process, the second goal of the game is to get as many autoclickers as possible!
